# Audio credits

All audio is stored locally in this folder so the website does not contact an external audio service during playback. The recordings have not been mixed with one another. Wikimedia Commons MP3 transcodes were used where available for broad mobile-browser support.

## Striped marsh frog page

- `striped-marsh-frog-call.m4a` — striped marsh frog (*Limnodynastes peronii*) recorded by **eli_hadwen**. Source: [iNaturalist sound 1962132](https://www.inaturalist.org/sounds/1962132), accessed through the [Atlas of Living Australia media record](https://images.ala.org.au/image/details?id=d613c4f6-3b68-43de-a07d-41651ee0ed39). [CC BY-NC 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/).
- `wetland-rain.mp3` — rain recorded by **ジダネ**. Source: [Wikimedia Commons, Rain.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rain.ogg). Public domain.
- `night-crickets.mp3` — night chorus of crickets and other orthopterans recorded by **Cory (ISAKA Yoji)**. Source: [Wikimedia Commons, Suzumushi 06z3286.ogg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Suzumushi_06z3286.ogg). [CC BY 2.1 JP](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.1/jp/deed.en).
